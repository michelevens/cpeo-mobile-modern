Mailing Address & Package Receiving

Commercial Mail Receiving Agency (CMRA)

As a registered Commercial Mail Receiving Agency (CMRA), CPEO accepts mail from the United States Postal Service for recipients, keeps it for collection, or re-mails it to your location with newly paid postage. We also serve as an Office Business Center (OBC) and Corporate Executive Center (CEC), providing secure mail and package handling alongside our full suite of office services.
