FAQ

Common questions.

Who uses CPEO?

Entrepreneurs, startups, consultants, attorneys, remote workers, and growing companies that need a professional presence without a traditional lease.

What are your hours?

Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM EST. Executive office tenants have extended access to their private offices.

Can I rent by the hour?

Yes. Conference rooms are available hourly, day offices by the day, and executive offices on monthly terms. We create plans tailored to your needs.

Do I need to schedule a room in advance?

We recommend booking in advance for boardrooms. Call 352-404-5955 or use the contact form to reserve your space.

Can you answer my phone calls?

Yes. Our receptionists provide personalized call answering with access to your company profile and custom greeting instructions.

Can I use your mailing address?

Yes. We're a registered Commercial Mail Receiving Agency (CMRA) offering mail collection, forwarding, and package receiving.
